What to Pack in Your Essentials Box

This box stays with YOU - don't put it on the truck. Include:

  • Medications for all family members
  • Phone chargers and basic electronics
  • Toiletries and personal care items
  • Change of clothes for each person
  • Snacks and water bottles
  • Important documents (IDs, contracts, medical records)
  • Basic cleaning supplies
  • Paper towels and toilet paper
  • Basic toolkit
  • Pet supplies if applicable
  • Children's favorite items/toys
  • First-night bedding essentials

Documents to Keep With You

  • Driver's licenses and IDs
  • Passports
  • Moving contract and inventory
  • Lease or closing documents for new home
  • Insurance documents
  • Medical and dental records
  • School records
  • Birth certificates, marriage license
  • Financial documents
  • Pet vaccination records
