Our Story

TopBudgetMove was founded with a simple mission: make long distance moving affordable and transparent. We saw an industry plagued by hidden fees, surprise charges, and unpredictable costs. Families were getting quoted one price and paying another. We knew there had to be a better way.

Our flat-rate model was born from a belief that moving shouldn't require a degree in contract law to understand. $7,000 for a full truck, anywhere in the continental US over 500 miles. That's it. No weight calculations, no distance multipliers, no fine print.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, transparent, and reliable long distance moving services that give families peace of mind during one of life's most stressful transitions.
