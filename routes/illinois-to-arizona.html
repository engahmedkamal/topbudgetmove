Chicago to Arizona: A Popular Migration

Illinois to Arizona is one of the top relocation routes from the Midwest. Thousands of Chicagoans make this move every year, trading harsh winters and high taxes for year-round sunshine and a lower cost of living.

Phoenix, Scottsdale, Tucson, and the surrounding areas offer everything from urban excitement to peaceful desert retirement communities. Whatever your Arizona dream, TopBudgetMove will get you there affordably.

Why Chicagoans Love Arizona

Escape the Cold

Arizona averages 299 sunny days per year. No more shoveling snow, scraping windshields, or layering up. Winter in Phoenix? Average high of 67°F.

Lower Taxes

Arizona has a flat income tax rate of 2.5% (compared to Illinois' 4.95%). Property taxes are also significantly lower.

Affordable Housing

Your housing dollar goes further in Arizona. The Phoenix metro offers diverse housing options at prices 20-30% below Chicago.

Outdoor Lifestyle

Hiking, golf, and outdoor activities year-round. The Grand Canyon, Sedona, and countless natural wonders are just a drive away.
