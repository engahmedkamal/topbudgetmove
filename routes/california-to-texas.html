What to Expect Moving from California to Texas

Climate Differences

Texas summers are hot and humid (especially in Houston). Winters are milder than Northern California but can surprise you. Be prepared for thunderstorms and occasional severe weather.

Cost of Living Savings

Expect to save 30-50% on housing costs depending on where you move. A $500,000 California home might cost $250,000-$350,000 in Texas for similar size.

Different Pace of Life

Texas has a more relaxed, friendly culture. People say "y'all" and wave to strangers. Traffic is different - more highways, more spread out cities.

Vehicle Registration

You'll need to register your vehicle in Texas within 30 days. Texas requires vehicle inspections and has different insurance requirements.

California to Texas Moving FAQ

How long does it take to move from California to Texas?

With TopBudgetMove's direct delivery, most California to Texas moves take 4-6 days from pickup to delivery, depending on the specific cities involved.

What's the best time to move from CA to TX?

Fall (September-November) and spring (March-May) offer the best weather for moving. Avoid summer if possible - Texas heat can be challenging for moving day.

Do I need to change my driver's license?

Yes, Texas requires new residents to obtain a Texas driver's license within 90 days of establishing residency.
