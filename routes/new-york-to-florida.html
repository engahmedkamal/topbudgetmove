Why New Yorkers Love Florida

No State Income Tax

Florida has no personal income tax. For high earners, this alone can save tens of thousands per year compared to New York's up to 10.9% state tax.

Lower Cost of Living

Housing costs are 30-50% lower than NYC metro. Property taxes are lower too. Your dollar stretches further in Florida.

Year-Round Sunshine

Say goodbye to brutal winters, snow shoveling, and seasonal depression. Florida averages 230+ sunny days per year.

Already Feels Like Home

Florida has huge New York transplant communities. You'll find NY-style pizza, bagels, and plenty of fellow New Yorkers.

New York to Florida Moving Tips

  • Update your address with USPS - Start mail forwarding before you leave
  • Florida driver's license - Required within 30 days of establishing residency
  • Vehicle registration - Must register your car in Florida within 10 days
  • Homestead exemption - Apply for property tax savings on your Florida home
  • Prepare for hurricanes - Hurricane season runs June-November; know your zone
  • AC maintenance - Your AC will work hard; have it serviced after arrival
