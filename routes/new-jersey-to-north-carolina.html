Why New Jerseyans Choose North Carolina

Massive Tax Savings

NJ has the highest property taxes in the nation. NC rates are 50-70% lower. Plus, NC's income tax (5.25%) beats NJ's top rate (10.75%).

Affordable Housing

The median home price in NC is roughly half of New Jersey's. Get twice the house for the same money.

Growing Job Market

Charlotte's banking sector, the Research Triangle's tech scene, and corporate relocations mean plenty of opportunities.

Four Mild Seasons

North Carolina offers real seasons without the harsh extremes. Mild winters, beautiful springs and falls.
