Cross Country Moving FAQ

How much does cross country moving cost?

With TopBudgetMove, cross country moving costs $7,000 flat rate for a full 26-foot truck. This covers any distance across the continental US - whether you're moving 1,000 miles or 3,000 miles.

How long does a cross country move take?

Most cross country moves take 7-10 days from pickup to delivery. The exact time depends on distance and road conditions. We'll provide an estimated delivery window when you book.

Should I drive or fly to my new home?

That's a personal choice! Many customers fly ahead to prepare their new home before delivery. Others drive alongside or behind the truck. We can coordinate delivery timing with your plans.

What should I pack in my essentials bag?

Pack items you'll need during transit: medications, important documents, phone chargers, toiletries, a change of clothes, and snacks. If driving, include items for the road trip.
