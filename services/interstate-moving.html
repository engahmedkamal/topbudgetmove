Why Using a Licensed Interstate Mover Matters

Not all moving companies can legally transport your belongings across state lines. Here's why licensing matters:

Federal Protection

Licensed interstate movers are regulated by the FMCSA. This means we must maintain proper insurance, follow safety standards, and provide you with specific consumer protections. If something goes wrong, you have federal recourse.

Insurance Requirements

We're required to carry minimum cargo insurance and liability coverage. This protects your belongings throughout the interstate journey. Unlicensed movers may have no coverage at all.

Written Estimates & Contracts

Federal law requires us to provide written estimates and contracts detailing services and costs. No verbal agreements or surprise charges.

Claims Process

If damage occurs, licensed movers must follow a regulated claims process. We have 30 days to acknowledge claims and 120 days to resolve them.

Interstate Moving Coverage

We're licensed to move you between any of the 48 continental United States. Whether you're moving from coast to coast or to a neighboring state, we've got you covered.

Interstate Moving FAQ

How do I verify TopBudgetMove is properly licensed?

You can verify our license on the FMCSA website at safer.fmcsa.dot.gov. All legitimate interstate moving companies are registered with the FMCSA and can provide their registration details upon request.

What documents will I receive for my interstate move?

You'll receive: a written estimate, a bill of lading (shipping contract), an inventory list of items, and "Your Rights and Responsibilities When You Move" booklet as required by federal law.

What happens if my belongings are damaged during interstate transport?

File a claim in writing within 9 months of delivery. We'll acknowledge your claim within 30 days and settle within 120 days. Standard coverage is $0.60 per pound per item. Full value protection is available.

Can I ship my car with my household goods?

We specialize in household goods. For vehicle transport, we can recommend trusted auto transport partners who will coordinate delivery with your move.
