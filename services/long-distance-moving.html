Frequently Asked Questions About Long Distance Moving

How far in advance should I book my long distance move?

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving season (May-September). However, we can often accommodate last-minute moves depending on availability.

What qualifies as a "long distance" move?

Any move over 500 miles qualifies for our flat-rate long distance pricing. For shorter moves, please contact us for a custom quote.

How long will my long distance move take?

Delivery times depend on distance: 500-1,000 miles typically takes 3-5 days; 1,000-2,000 miles takes 5-7 days; over 2,000 miles takes 7-10 days. Our direct delivery ensures faster transit than companies that consolidate shipments.

Do I need to be present for loading and unloading?

Yes, someone over 18 must be present at both pickup and delivery to sign paperwork and direct furniture placement. If you can't be there, you can designate a representative.
